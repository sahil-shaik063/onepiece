    Tony Tony Chopper

    • overview
    • users
    • users
    • comments
    Gender: Male
    Hair Color: Brown
    Rank #129
    Rank #519

    Often seen as the Straw Hat’s pet or mascot by outsiders, Chopper is actually the onboard doctor. He is a blue-nosed reindeer from Drum Island who felt that he lost his place in the world after eating the Human-human fruit and gaining the ability to do anything that a person can. He aims to become a brilliant doctor who can cure any illness, though he doesn’t fare particularly well in the heat. Chopper hasn’t quite mastered the art of hiding behind objects or accepting compliments, but he will get quite annoyed if you mistake him for a Tanuki.
